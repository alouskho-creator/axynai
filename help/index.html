Nexa AI – Help & Support

Welcome to Nexa AI. This page provides help with accounts, sign-in, and basic app usage.

1. Account and Sign-In

You can create an account using:

  • Email and password
  • Google Sign-In
  • Apple Sign-In (when available)

Use a valid email address to receive important account messages.

2. I didn’t receive a verification email

  • Check your spam or junk folder
  • Wait a few minutes and refresh your inbox
  • Confirm the email address was entered correctly

3. I forgot my password

To reset your password:

  1. Go to the login screen
  2. Select “Forgot password”
  3. Enter your email address
  4. Follow the instructions sent to your email

4. Uploading notes

You can upload images of handwritten or printed notes.

  • Use clear images
  • Ensure good lighting
  • Avoid blurry or cropped photos

5. Organizing notes

Nexa AI organizes notes only when you press the “Organize notes” button. No automatic processing occurs.

6. Data and privacy

Nexa AI processes user content only to provide requested features. See our Privacy Policy for details.

7. Supported platforms

  • Web
  • Android (coming soon)
  • iOS (coming soon)

8. Contact support

If you need help, contact us at support@nexaai.app.
